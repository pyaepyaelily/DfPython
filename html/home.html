About our Website

Our website's purpose is to allow users to look for desired type of food like, halal or vegetarian. We have functions like the food filter which helps to filter the choices that the user wants. It makes it easier for them to find what they want with the restaurant finder and also, the users can find out honest reviews about the restaurants given by former customers of the restaurants. The bmi calculator helps users who are concerned about their fitness to check their bmi and the healthy bmi range. We also have a chatroom which users can use to chat with each other and find out opinions about food.
